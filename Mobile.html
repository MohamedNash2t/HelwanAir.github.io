MOBILE CHECK IN
Check in to your EGYPTAIR flights from anywhere! simply type egyptair.com on your mobile devices web browser and select the
check-in option or on EGYPTAIR mobile application. You will receive your Elevtronic Boarding Pass directly on to your mobile
device.

General Conditions of This Service

  • Mobile Check-In accessible from 48 hours before departure, and up until 90 minutes before your flights
    scheduled departure time.
  • For passengers holding electronic tickets with confirmed reservations on flights operated by EGYPTAIR
  • Passengers carrying approved vabin baggage only can proceed directly to the boarding gate
  • Passengers with check-in baggage, the bags will have to be presented at the Special EGYPTAIR 'Baggage drop'
    dedicated counters well in advance of the boarding time (at least one hour) proir to departure time.
  • For all other changes , including destination, changes for unaccompained minors or in the case that changes
    are not required for all passengers , please contact nearest EGYPTAIR office or AEGYPTAIR call center at 1717
